GIGANTIC gene trees README file.

This directory contains scripts used to generate GIGANTIC gene tree analyses of Species19 genomes for the Aplysia genome paper. 

The work was done by Jan Hsaio and Lola Chenxi Deng from summer 2020 to winter 2022, both in part as interns at the Salk Institute in the Chalasani Lab, and by Eric Edsinger summer 2019 to fall 2022 as a research scientist at the Salk Institute in the Chalasani Lab and fall 2022 as a visiting researcher at the University of Washington's Friday Harbor Laboratories marine field station.
